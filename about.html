About Panda Prep 🐼

Panda Prep is a free, friendly place to practise for competitive exams. We focus on the three things common to most exams: Aptitude, General Knowledge and English.

Every topic has three levels — easy, medium and hard — so you can start gently and build up. Answers stay hidden until you tap the panda, so you can think first and check after.

Our goal is simple: make daily practice calm, clear and a little bit fun. Practice daily, grow steadily.
